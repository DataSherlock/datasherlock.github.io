Data Warehousing

ETL Technologies, Scripting, Data Models, Framework, Data Quality

I have over 9 years of experience building ETL workflows and designing data models. I have worked on a wide variety of client engagements ranging from designing/building enterprise data warehouses to complex data marts. I've played a key role in building the underlying framework using UNIX shell scripting. I have experience in working through the entire SDLC and have very recently been working on Agile projects as well. From a management POV, I am adept at estimation and can help you write up responses to RFPs.

    Robotic Process Automation

    As a software engineer, all the expectations that a client has of me can be summed up in one simple statement - "Has my work made the client's life easier?". While data analytics and data warehousing have opened up new frontiers in exploration and decision making, RPA is a step towards diverting monotonous and mechanical tasks to robots and giving humans the time and energy to focus on using their judgment and subjectivity towards everyday work. My expertise in the RPA can help you automate a lot of your day to day, mundane but important tasks.
